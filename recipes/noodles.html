Noodles


Description

Quick and savory stir-fried noodles loaded with vegetables and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (any variety)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make noodles

  1. Boil the noodles as per the package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a wok or pan, then add ginger and garlic. Stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender but crisp.
  4. Toss in the noodles, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix everything thoroughly.
  5. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
