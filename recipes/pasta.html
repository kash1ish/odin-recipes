Pasta


Description

A delicious, creamy tomato-based pasta dish perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps to make pasta

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, ensuring even coating.
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese and garnish with fresh basil. Serve hot!
